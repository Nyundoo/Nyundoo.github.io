Who we are

SUNIAL AND DIA HOLDINGS LTD IS A GENERAL SUPPLIES YOUTH enterprise incorporated in the Republic of Kenya on 23th day of June 2015. The main objective of this Company is to offer and deliver high quality goods and services to customer institutions, departments and companies. The Company deals in supply of consumables and non-consumables.
Sunial and Dia Holdings Limited has a well laid out platform to achieve its goals, to the satisfaction of its clients.

Our Mission
